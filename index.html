Encanto: Grandchildren of the Madrigals.

Images Info & their Gifts Struggles

Mirabel, the Youngest of the Julieta and Agustin children in the Madrigal family tree, was eager to receive her magical gift but was disappointed when her door failed to light up. The family believed she was giftless, leaving her feeling isolated and misunderstood.

Mirabel felt isolated from her gifted family as she desperately tried to help but only seemed to create more problems. Observing the cracks in the Casita and seeing her presence in Bruno's vision, she realized that she held a key role in the future. She understood that she had to fix whatever was causing the Casita to break, before it was too late and the situation worsened.
Isabela, the Olddest of Julieta and Agustin children in the Madrigal family tree, She recieved the Gift of Nature and could grow roses, plants, and trees to make everything beautiful and perfect. She was responsible to look beautiful and be perfect at all time, which she felt pressured to be perfect and not be herself. She was also in arranged marriage with Mariano but didn't go against the decision, she only agreed for the sake of the family.
Luisa, the Second Olddest of Julieta and Agustin children in the Madrigal family tree, She recieved the Gift of Strength, ability to carry buildings and all heavy animals at ease. She felt pressured in many responsibilities she has to carry as people needed her a lot to move heavy objects or buildings in the community. Making her even more pressured as if the world is on her shoulders and wants to relax for once.
Antonio, the Youngest of Pepa and Felix childrens in the Madrigal family, He recently recieve the Gift of Animals, able to communicate and guide them what to do. He was afraid that He won't able to live up to expectation in his gift ceremony, knowing how Abuela wants the best of the family and gifts that help us all.
Dolores, the Olddest of Pepa and Felix Children in the Madrigal Family, She recieved a Gift of Enhanced hearing, able to hear things miles away and even the quietest sound, she can also hear. She felt bothered that, she was able to hear everything, even the deepest secret that is told, and She can't keep a secret. It also bothers her a lot as she often can't sleep well with her hearing, especially during events or fireworks.
Camilo, the Second Oldest of Pepa and Felix children in the Madrigal family tree, He recieved the Gift of Shapeshifing, able to transform into another person to another. He was also pressured to help the communities, he often hide his feeling such as fear or insecurities by being cheerful and joke around. He also had issue with himself as he often don't know who he is yet.
